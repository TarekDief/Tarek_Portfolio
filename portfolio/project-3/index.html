Quad-rotor

Quad-rotor is one of the most famous drones, it is a VTOL (Vertical Take-Off and Landing) aircraft which could be used in many applications like military surveillance, agriculture services which require high level of safety with low cost. However, it needs a complicated control strategy to overcome the uncertainty comes from wind and mathematical model.

My main responsibilities:

  • Developed non-linear mathematical model.
  • Autonomous control (PID & Fuzzy).
  • Adaptive Flight-Path Control for the kite system
  • Build full system from scratch.
