Kite power system.

The aim of kite power system is to demonstrate a new technology to harvest wind energy with substantially lower material and environmental footprints with higher capacity factor than conventional wind turbines.

My main responsibilities:

  • Derive a full mathematical model for the kite including the kite surface and tether.
  • Stimulate the mathematical model in Simulink/Matlab software.
  • Apply system estimation algorithms to identify the system parameters.
  • Design and implement a control algorithm for the derived model.
  • Build a full system from scratch.
  • Testing and tune the control in real-flight tests.
