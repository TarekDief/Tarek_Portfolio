ABOUT Me

I am an energetic, results-oriented, Aeronautical Engineer. Currently, I am an airplane development engineer in PD Aerospace company (on leave), and postdoctoral researcher at University of Alberta, Canada. I received a PhD degree from Kyushu University in Fukuoka, Japan, and completed MSc and BSc degrees in Aeronautical and Aerospace Engineering from Cairo University, Egypt.

My research interests include guidance, control, embedded, and dynamics systems especially related to drone, robotics, and renewable energy systems.

For a long time, I have been using a lot of computer tools, engineering programs, and programming languages. When I face a problem, I could learn some tools quickly and solve the problem or generate some results.

The important thing is that I could learn in a fast way and get the job done ;)
